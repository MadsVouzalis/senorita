Debbi Phipps Creator of Tropical
SEÑORITA LIMÓNITA Cocktail Mix

One hot Arizona summer, my friends and I were drinking margaritas and EVERYONE was complaining about the same thing - including me - and that was heartburn! I decided they tasted so good that I would create a margarita that didn't do that to you. It took three and a half months and I blew up three $100 blenders in the process (It was a fun summer, by the way!). One day, I ran out of some of the fruits I was using and blended some ingredients I had on hand. I witnessed something I had never seen before - people's eyes got wide and their faces lit up. They responded with "delicious, smooth and refreshing." They took one sip and said "I feel like I'm on vacation." This unique concoction had the WOW factor!

Over the years, it became my personal claim to fame. I made margaritas everywhere I went and listened to demands that this flavor be bottled and on the shelves for consumers to enjoy.

I formed a corporation in April 2010 and started working on the entire concept - coming up with a product name, creating a registered trademark logo, researching manufacturers, etc. Not having the money in my budget to hire a spokesperson, I created my own. SEÑORITA LIMÓNITA™ made her first playful appearance on Facebook in June of 2010. Through her, I entered the world of social networking, not only promoting her, but unexpectedly inspiring others in the process.

As her social media presence grew, SEÑORITA LIMÓNITA™ began receiving requests to promote her amazing mix in person at special events. Through an astounding story, it became necessary for me to BECOME the physical icon personality of SEÑORITA LIMÓNITA™, an official Ujena bikini model, runner, and golfer. I can't wait to see what happens next!

ROCKY LIMONITA

3 shots Senorita Limonita mix, 1 shot water, 1 shot quality 100% blue agave reposado tequila. Add ice and blend in a cocktail shaker.

SENORITA RUMMER

"For the Pirate in Us All"

3 shots Senorita Limonita mix, 1 shot rum (banana, coconut or other flavored rums are excellent!) Add an equal amount of ice and blend in a high quality blender.

DESERT EXOTIC LIMONITA

1-1/2 oz. Senorita Limonita mix, 1 oz. Prickly Pear Syrup, 1 oz. tequila, 1 oz. Grand Marnier and blend with an equal amount of ice.

SENORITA PEARL

Prepare a frozen Limonita and add nature's best frozen snack, Tropical Fruit Pearls. These tiny pearls of explosive flavor create an amazingly delicious drink!

WINOLIMONITA

Instead of rum or tequiila, add 1 shot of German Riesling, preferably Spatlese. This is the reduced calorie version! Drink more of these by the pool without the high alcohol content. Red wine lovers, add Jam Jar Shiraz!

POLARITA

3 shots Senorita Mix with 1 shot Polish Vodka. Blend in a high quality blender with an equal amount of ice.

LIMOSA

1/3 Senorita Limonita mix and 2/3 Champagne!

ARNOLIMONITA

CAFELIMONITA
